What is Kappa Sigma?

​The ​Kappa Sigma Fraternity was founded at the University of Bologna in 1400 while the American founding was on December 10, 1869 at the University of Virginia. Today Kappa Sigma boasts over 350 collegiate chapters with 170,000 living alumni and is the most preferred fraternity in the world. Kappa Sigma develops young men into gentlemen through the four pillars of Fellowship, Leadership, Scholarship, and Service.​

Fellowship

Joining Kappa Sigma provides the opportunity for anyone to build relationships with brothers that will last a lifetime. With over 230,000 men that have been initiated it is impossible to not find a brother wherever you may go, creating a bond with not only your chapter, but the national organization as well. Joining Kappa Sigma will help you to build an extended family and establish a vast array of professional contacts.

Leadership

Kappa Sigmas are developed into leaders through their experiences and accomplishments they achieve during their time as undergraduates and throughout their whole lives. Distinguished brothers, from Ted Turner to Bob Dole, have developed into the leaders they are through their journey as a Kappa Sigma.

Academics

Academics are the primary focus of all Kappa Sigmas, especially at our chapter, the Alpha Delta chapter. Brothers are always willing to lend a hand when it comes to school work, tutoring, or advice on majors and career paths. Every week library hours are held where brothers in our fraternity study or finish homework and are willing to help each other. Being a Kappa Sigma you have the opportunity to apply for scholarships through the Kappa Sigma Endowment Fund which offers almost $1,000,000 worth of scholarships each year to brothers who excel in scholarship and leadership.

Service

Brothers always willing to help others in the community and each year put in thousands of hours of community service whether its through blood drives, charity sports games or concerts, or park clean ups. Our Fraternity is actively involved in THON in which we raised over $30,000 dollars for in the 2012-2013 school year. Many projects are implemented through "Kappa Sigma: A Greater Cause"​ which is "our bold initiative to promote volunteerism, citizenship and service to the community." Kappa Sigma also raises thousands of dollars each year for its premier philanthropy, "The Military Heroes Campaign."

Local History

Our Alpha Delta Chapter was founded on January 8th 1892. A new colony of Alpha Delta was created on April 28th, 2007, and soon after our chapter was re-instated on April 18th, 2009. Because of our long history we have a vast network of alumni that have come through our chapter and still keep in touch as well as visit from time to time. Through out the years our chapter has won many awards and we are diligently working to become the number one fraternity at The Pennsylvania State University.
